15th & 16th May 2023 Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

THE DIGITAL CITIES SUMMIT & EXPO 2023

The centerpiece of Saudi Arabia’s giga project ecosystem and as per the vision 2030’s economic diversification programme, NEOM is a fully digitized city which is being developed with an investment of $500 billion and is one of the most renowned projects globally. The construction market will contribute significantly to the nations GDP by 2030 and create a host of job opportunities across the nation. NEOM will comprise of four major projects, SINDALAH, OXAGON, THE LINE & TROJENA. The Kingdom of Saudi of Saudi Arabia will also be developing the “Prince Mohammed bin Salman Non-Profit City”, that will be a model for the development of the non-profit sector globally and an incubator for youth and volunteer groups as well as local and international non-profit institutions. One of the most recent developments announced will be the development of the AlNama Smart City which will be a zero-carbon city.

The Digital Cities Summit will bring together international smart city experts, key government and private smart city stakeholders, smart city technology providers, buyers, influencers and investors on one platform to meet, network, share their views and perspectives in order to move the nation towards being a global leader in the smart city sphere. This event will feature a host of very exclusive case study presentations, technical workshops, panel discussions, product launches, technology showcase, live product demos, business meetings and networking opportunities.

With billion-dollar investments going into the development of smart cities in Saudi Arabia, the region presents a host of sustainable business opportunities for both local and international smart city stakeholders.
