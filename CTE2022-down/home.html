The Evolution of Smart & Digital Construction Technologies

About Construction Technology Summit

The Construction Technology Excellence Summit & Expo is a two day knowledge and business exchange platform, which is being hosted by Emerging Markets Middle East on 9th & 10th June 2022 with a prime focus on Post Covid-19 strategies and technologies that can help fast track construction projects across India. The country will be the third largest construction market in the world by 2022 with multi billion dollar investments in excess of US$ 777 billion on sustainable infrastructure development projects that present a host of business opportunities.

With a key focus on the development of smart cities, housing projects, commercial properties, industrial cities, healthcare cities, leisure & hospitality projects, financial districts, municipal projects, airport and airport cities, ports, transportation and infrastructure projects. This event will provide all stake holders with unlimited educational insights and excellent business exchange opportunities.

The Construction Technology Excellence Summit & Expo will feature a series of case study presentations, highly interactive panel discussions, technology driven workshops led by our panel of globally renowned construction experts and technology providers, product launches, live product demos or technology showcase and an innovation hub to help achieve excellence across India’s construction sector.
