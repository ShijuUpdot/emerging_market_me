About Digital Cities Summit

“Transforming Saudi Arabia's Cities into Safe, Sustainable, Digitized Cities of the Future”

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is looking at Smart Cities as one of the key drivers for economic diversification that will contribute to the nation’s economy significantly by 2030. Saudi Arabia has already allocated $500 billion which will be invested across 285 municipalities to implement smart cities, and have already initiated efforts to transform the first five cities, including Makkah, Riyadh, Jeddah, Al- Madinah and Al-Ahsa.

The centerpiece of Saudi Arabia’s giga project ecosystem and as per the vision 2030’s economic diversification programme, Neom is a fully digitized city which is being developed with an investment of $500 billion and is one of the most renowned projects globally. The construction market will contribute significantly to the nations GDP by 2030 and create a host of job opportunities across the nation.

We at Emerging Markets Middle East will be hosting a virtual event known as the Digital Cities Summit on 15th & 16th November 2021. This two-day event is being hosted in line with the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia’s vision to develop fully digitized cities that are intelligent, safe and sustainable.

The Digital Cities Summit will bring together international smart city experts, key government and private smart city stakeholders, smart city technology providers, buyers, influencers and investors on one virtual platform to meet, network, share their views and perspectives in order to move the nation towards being a global leader in the smart city sphere. This event will feature some very exclusive case study presentations, technology presentations, technical workshops, panel discussions, business meetings and networking opportunities.

WHY SAUDI ARABIA?

Saudi Arabia is geographically well positioned as a country to create a new digitalized global city, that will be situated at the crossroad of three continents Asia, Europe and Africa with half the world's population accessible within a five-hour flight. The country is already a key state in the MENAT region. Saudi Arabia spends Billions per year in ICT investment and that doesn't account for the corporate and foreign investment the Kingdom's digital revolution attracts.

As a key regional leader in the development of next generation solutions and technology, the country presents lucrative investment opportunities for international investors. Technologies such as 5G Mobile Networks, BIM, 3D Printing, BAS, ITS, Smart Energy Management Systems, Smart Metering, Renewable Energy, Fintech, Artificial intelligence, Virtual Reality, Robotics, Autonomous Vehicles, Drones, Biometrics and Low-cost space exploration and micro-satellites will play a very vital role in the development of smart and sustainable cities across Saudi Arabia.
